Privacy Policy for KenGPT Chrome Extension

Effective Date: July 2025

The KenGPT Chrome Extension ("KenGPT", "we", "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This policy explains how the extension works and what data it does (and doesn’t) collect.

What KenGPT Does

  • Reads property listing information from Zillow or Redfin that you manually view in your browser
  • Extracts public listing data like address, price, and description from the page
  • Sends this data and your follow-up questions to OpenAI’s API to generate property analysis

What KenGPT Does Not Do

  • We do NOT collect or store any personally identifiable information (PII)
  • We do NOT track your browsing history
  • We do NOT transmit any personal data to our servers (there are none)
  • We do NOT use cookies or any form of user fingerprinting

Third-Party APIs

KenGPT uses the OpenAI API to process and analyze listing information. Only listing text and your questions are sent securely to OpenAI’s servers. OpenAI may log this usage under their own privacy policy.

Security

KenGPT processes all data locally in your browser or securely via HTTPS to the OpenAI API. It does not transmit data to any server operated by the extension developer.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
